About Us

Crafting Champions Uniting players, Creating memories, Inspiring excellence.

Welcome to Next-Gen Sports Association, a passionate community built by Sports enthusiasts for Sports enthusiasts.

We have successfully organized and hosted two consecutive seasons of badminton tournaments, bringing together players from diverse backgrounds, levels, and age groups. These events not only showcased incredible talent and sportsmanship but also strengthened the spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition within our community.
